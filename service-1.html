Program & Project Management

Program/Project Management

Lyseum Partners delivers end-to-end program and project management services designed to support the unique demands of government initiatives. With a team experienced in leading multi-million-dollar, multi-stakeholder projects, Lyseum ensures mission alignment, accountability, and on-time performance from planning through execution.

Construction & Infrastrucute Management

Construction Management

Our construction management services are built on military-grade discipline and commercial excellence. Lyseum Partners provides oversight that ensures quality, compliance, and safety in the delivery of infrastructure projects in complex, secure, and remote environments.

Logistic Support

We specialize in the logistics required for government and defense operations—ensuring timely delivery, resource coordination, and operational readiness in support of infrastructure and mission-critical projects.

Master Planning

Lyseum supports government infrastructure growth through comprehensive master planning services. We help agencies develop scalable, sustainable, and secure plans for current and future facility and site requirements.

Engineering & Technical Services

Engineering Design and Inspections

Our engineering design and inspection services support the U.S. Government’s need for precision and reliability in infrastructure projects. From concept to compliance, our team ensures every design meets regulatory, safety, and performance standards

Engineering Compliance Support

With deep expertise in regulatory frameworks and international codes, Lyseum ensures that all projects meet engineering compliance standards. This reduces risk, enhances safety, and guarantees successful project certification for federal and defense clients.

Cost Estimating and Surveys

Our cost estimating and surveying services provide federal clients with accurate, transparent data to support budget forecasting, contract planning, and lifecycle management—ensuring financial efficiency and informed decision-making.

Facility Condition Assessments

We offer comprehensive facility condition assessments to help government clients maintain mission readiness, optimize asset performance, and prioritize capital investments across their infrastructure portfolios.

Scheduling & Quality Control

Project Scheduling and Submittal Review

Lyseum excels in creating and managing project schedules that align with government protocols and operational timelines. Our submittal review process ensures that all documentation meets stringent government standards and supports streamlined project delivery.

Safety & Security Consulting

Safety Consulting

We provide safety consulting services that uphold the highest standards of occupational health and risk mitigation, supporting military and government operations in environments that demand zero tolerance for error.

Anti-Terrorism and Force Protection (ATFP) Measures

Lyseum supports mission assurance through the integration of ATFP strategies into facility planning and construction. Our team has the background and access to implement protective measures that align with Department of Defense requirements and homeland security priorities.
